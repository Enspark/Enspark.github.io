FAQ

Everything you should know about the camp

Who is this bootcamp for?

Teenagers (Year 9/PT3 Level Math) with an interest to discover or explore coding.

Do I need to have coding experience?

Not at all. All you need is Year 9/PT3 Level Math, and an interest in programming.

Should I sign up if I'm not sure if I have any interest in coding?

This is perfectly normal as coding is a relatively new expertise.
We strongly encourage you to sign up for a one day free trial to find out if it's worth pursuing.

What are the course times?

9AM -4PM daily from Monday - Saturday for 2 weeks.

What if I miss some days or unable to commit full time daily?

Learning process is personalized. Our instructors will try their best to help you catch up to your expectations.

Will I be fully capable to develop a website after the course?

Yes. After completion of the front end development camp, you will be able to develop websites.
